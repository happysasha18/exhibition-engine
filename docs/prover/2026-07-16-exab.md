## What I assumed
- `config.experiments` is baked into the served bundle (synchronously available at boot); E2 concerns
  deal-vs-beat ordering within the load, not a late config fetch.
- `ex.visitor` is one `localStorage` key holding the coat-check token (memory on) or a per-tab id
  (memory off); E6 reads the "per tab" wording against that single-key model and against INV-13.
